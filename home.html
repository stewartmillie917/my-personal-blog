About Me

Hello! My name is Jones Wilson, and I'm a passionate web developer, creative thinker, and lifelong learner based in the Netherlands. With a strong foundation in front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites that make a real impact.

I started my journey into web development out of curiosity — one line of code turned into a full website, and soon I was hooked. Since then, I've been constantly improving my skills, exploring modern frameworks, and designing digital experiences that blend functionality with aesthetics.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the beautiful canals of Amsterdam, reading about emerging tech trends, or enjoying a good cup of coffee while planning my next project. I believe in the power of simplicity, attention to detail, and writing code that not only works — but tells a story.

This personal site is my digital home — a place to showcase my work, share my thoughts, and connect with like-minded individuals. Whether you're here to collaborate, learn, or just say hi, I'm glad you're here!
